Face filters for Instagram and Facebook

Have you ever used the Instagram or Facebook Messenger camera and used one of their face filters? Did you know YOU can design and publish your own? Right now that is the primary service we provide. We plan to offer virtual reality content such as 360 photo tours and more in the near future.

1. Design

We help you design a fun, custom face filter and help you publish it to Facebook or Instagram.

2. Discover

We will create a QR code to print on stickers or pomotional material. Scanning the code automatically launches the filter.

3. Capture

Customers use the filter in Instagram/Facebook story posts or even in video chat!

4. Share

When people encounter your filter from a customer's post, they have the opportunity to use and share it. This can generate tens of thousands of encounters!
